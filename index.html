Biography

I am an MLOps Engineer at Verda, where I deploy, scale, and maintain AI models in production. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Helsinki, supervised by Professor Kai Puolamäki from the Exploratory Data Analysis group. My PhD was supervised by Associate Professor Michael Mathioudakis from the Algorithmic Data Science group. My PhD thesis is titled “Scaling and Maintaining Machine Learning Pipelines”.
